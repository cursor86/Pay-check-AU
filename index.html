Understanding the 2026 Australian Tax Reforms

From July 1, 2025, the Stage 3 Tax Cuts and 12% Superannuation Guarantee have redefined take-home pay. Middle earners now benefit from a lower 16% base rate and a widened 30% bracket. Additionally, the shift to Marginal HECS Repayments provides vital relief for graduates by calculating debt repayments only on income earned above the threshold.
