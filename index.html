Understanding Your 2026 Pay Slip

The 2025-26 Australian financial year introduces the most significant tax reforms in a decade. By lowering the 19% bracket to 16% and capping the middle bracket at 30% for incomes up to $135,000, the government has increased disposable income for millions. Furthermore, the 12% Superannuation Guarantee ensures your future self is protected, while the shift to Marginal HECS Repayments ensures that pay rises no longer result in disproportionately high debt repayments.
